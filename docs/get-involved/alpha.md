## Focus Areas

- Contest Setup: selecting definitions, entering operator details, start time.
- QSO Entry: band/mode inference, exchange validation, duplicate/rework detection.
- Log Management: search/sort, duplicates visibility, editing.
- Scoring: score bar behavior, multipliers, segment totals.
- Export: ADIF/Cabrillo previews and Share Sheet.

## Expectations

- Short, frequent builds; features may change quickly.
- Report issues with clear reproduction steps and screenshots.
