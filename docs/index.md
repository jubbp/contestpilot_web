## Welcome to ContestPilot

Since becoming a HAM a few years ago, I was struck by the lack of support fo Apple operating systems in Amateur Radio.

ContestPilot is the first of my attempts to redress this imbalance.

ContestPilot is a contest logging software

## Get Involved

ContestPilot is currently in development and closed alpha testing. In the near future we wil release a closed beta version fo testing with a larger testing group.
