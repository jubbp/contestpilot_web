## Browsing Your Log

- Search by callsign, filter duplicates, and sort (newest/oldest/callsign).
- iPhone shows cards; iPad presents a table view for dense review.

## Editing QSOs

- Tap a QSO to open the edit sheet.
- Adjust fields; validation applies on save.

## Duplicates Handling

- Toggle visibility of dupes; reworks are indicated when allowed by rules.
