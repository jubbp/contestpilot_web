## Closed Alpha

Contest Pilot is currently in closed Alpha testing. I you would like to become part of the testing team, please send an email to [info@contestpilot.app](mailto:info@contestpilot.app)

## Getting Started

Once you have requested and been approved for the testing team, you will need to install the Testflight app on your device(s). After acceptance you will recieve an invitation to joing Testflight for Contest Pilot.

Follow the instructions in the email or email [info@contestpilot.app](mailto:info@contestpilot.app) with any issues.
